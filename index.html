Search and add your expires to this dairy. Introducing "ExpiryTracker," a convenient and user-friendly app designed to help you stay organized and avoid wasting food or other perishable items. With ExpiryTracker, you can effortlessly manage the expiry dates of your items and ensure that nothing goes to waste.

Features

  • Expiry List at Your Fingertips: ExpiryTracker provides a centralized and easily accessible list of all your items with expiry dates right on your home screen. Never forget about an expiring product again!
  • Smart Notifications: Stay on top of your inventory with customizable reminders. ExpiryTracker will send you timely notifications to ensure you consume or discard items before they expire.
  • Effortless Item Entry: Adding new items to your list is a breeze. Simply input the name of the item and its corresponding expiry date, and ExpiryTracker will automatically sort it for you.
  • Intuitive Organization: ExpiryTracker arranges your items in a visually appealing and intuitive manner, allowing you to quickly scan and identify items that are close to their expiration dates.
  • Search and Filter: With a robust search and filter functionality, you can easily find specific items or sort them based on various parameters such as name, category, or remaining days until expiry.
  • Custom Categories: Categorize your items for better organization and ease of navigation. Whether it's groceries, medication, or household products, ExpiryTracker lets you create personalized categories to suit your needs.
  • Data Backup and Sync: Your data is precious, and ExpiryTracker ensures its safety. With built-in backup and sync capabilities, you can seamlessly access and manage your item list across multiple devices.
  • User-Friendly Interface: ExpiryTracker boasts a clean and user-friendly interface, making it simple for anyone to navigate and utilize its features. No technical expertise required!
